Step 1 — Image

Upload your photo

Click the upload zone or drag and drop any image file. Once loaded, a palette of colours will be automatically extracted from the image to suggest background colours for your caption.

1
Click the dashed upload area in the Image panel on the left, or drag a file onto it.
2
After upload, a framing slider appears — use it to pan left/right or up/down to frame the shot perfectly.
3
Supports JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC and most common formats.
Step 2 — Caption

Add your caption text

Fill in the city, location, and time fields to build your caption. An optional notes line lets you add a short description below the main heading.

City
e.g. Sydney — the main heading line
Place
e.g. Bondi Beach — shown after a comma
Time
e.g. 07:15 AM — appended with an em dash
Note
Optional — a softer second line of text
Step 3 — Style

Customise the look

Open the Style panel to fine-tune every visual detail. All changes update the preview instantly.

🎨
Background colour — pick from extracted palette swatches or use the + button for any custom colour.
↕️
Caption position — toggle between top and bottom placement.
📐
Aspect ratio — choose 3:4, 1:1, 4:3, or 9:16 for the right platform.
🔤
Font style — Serif, Sans, Mono, or Italic.
💧
Text opacity — subtly fade the text for a layered look.
Step 4 — Export

Render and download

When you're happy with the live preview, hit Render High-Res to produce the final image at full resolution. Then download as JPG, PNG, or copy it directly to your clipboard.

1
The live preview updates in real time — it's a fast draft at screen resolution.
2
Click Render High-Res to produce a full-resolution output matching your original photo's pixel dimensions.
3
Use Download JPG for sharing, PNG for transparency-safe archiving, or Copy to paste directly.
